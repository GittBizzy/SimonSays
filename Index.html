Tutorial

Hello and welcome to Simon Says, a fun and interactive memory game! Simon Says is a circular gameboard with four buttons on top. Each button lights up and plays a sound when pressed. Try and follow the pattern demonstrated at the beginning of each round. Simon Says will speed up as the game progresses so it becomes more difficult to win. Play with up to 2 players.
